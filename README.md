# Customer-churn-analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn data and patterns to identify key factors influencing customer retention.

## Objective
To understand which customer segments are most likely to churn and provide actionable business recommendations.

## Key insights
- Customers in their first 12 months of lifecycle have the highest churn rate.
- Churn decreases as tenure increases.
- Customers with frequent support calls shows higher churn rate.

## Recommendations
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Proactively support customers with high support interactions.
- Use early churn signals to prevent customer loss.

## Dataset
Customers churn dataset (Kaggle)
